Rajasthan Crime Cell

The officers and men of Raj Police Department are committed to providing professional law enforcement services, protecting the rights of individuals, preventing crime, and building community partnership.

We will serve the community with integrity, diversity, and quality and to provide proactive interaction with the community to enhance the feeling of safety and security in the state.
